DontPanicTV is a documentary series specializing in independent musicians and artists all over the globe. DPTV is your ticket to the hottest indie rock bands performing live at your fingertips, created to connect fans with up-and-coming artists through stunning live show recordings, in-depth interviews, studio and tour video blogs, and much more. We are committed to exposing the incredible energy and intimate personalities of each musician through easy-to-access, exciting documentaries.
